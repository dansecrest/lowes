Appearance

From the frame to the finish, your interior doors can be customized to reflect your personal style.

Consider your interior door’s appearance

Color

Find color options that complement your home’s design—and your personal style. It’s up to you whether you’d like your doors to blend into the wall or stand out.

Frame

Match your home’s architectural style with the perfect door trim. A variety of casings (visible frames) and corner blocks (carved blocks in upper corners) can be carefully fashioned as part of a comprehensive pre-hanging package.
