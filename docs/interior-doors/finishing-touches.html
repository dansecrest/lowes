Hardware

Choosing the right hardware comes down to style and function. Going for a contemporary look? Think clean lines and simple hardware. Are rustic doors more your style? Add some clavos (decorative nails), hinge tips or metallic inlays for an old-world look.

*Product availability varies upon region.

Router carved touches

The distinctive dimensional details of router carved panels can elevate the entire look of your room. They come in raised, planked, or flat profiles and can even be matched to existing styles.

*Product availability varies upon region.

Factory finish
