Safety

Hopefully you never have to worry about a fire in your home, but it’s always better to be prepared.

Here’s what to know about safety.

Fire-rated doors

The purpose of a fire-rated door is not only to keep fire out but to give you time to leave or get help. A fire rating tells you how long the door takes to burn. Your options range from 20 to 90 minutes, depending on the door.
