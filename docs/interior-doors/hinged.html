Hinged

Hinged

This swing-open, swing-shut style is a traditional choice for an interior door. You can buy as door only or a pre-hung unit.

Here are your hinged door options

Single pre-hung doors

Pre-hung doors come attached to the frame with all the necessary hinges and handles. A single door can be an understated transition from room to room, or a discreet enclosure for a closet or storage.

*Product availability varies upon region.

Double pre-hung doors

Great as an elegant entry to a study, dining area or anywhere you’d like to make a statement. Double doors can also bring more light into a room and unify the space.

*Product availability varies upon region.

French doors

In the 17th century, French doors started out as floor-to-ceiling windows that doubled as doors. Nowadays, they’re still an elegant and practical way to maximize space and sunlight.

*Product availability varies upon region.
