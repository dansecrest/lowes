Wood

Wood interior doors are crafted to complement your home’s architectural style and personal aesthetic.

Why choose wood interior doors?

Strength & beauty

Solid wood doors have been the standard for centuries—the high end look that other materials imitate. It’s strong and secure yet warm and inviting, with distinctive color and variation in each species.

*Product availability varies upon region.

Lasting durability

Wear and tear are no match for solid wood. Compared to other materials, it’s the least likely to dent and scratches are easy to repair.

*Product availability varies upon region.

Quality & craftsmanship

Centuries of craftsmanship have been passed down and improved upon in today’s interior doors. With professionally crafted styles and custom wood options, you’re bound to find the look you’re after.

*Product availability varies upon region.
