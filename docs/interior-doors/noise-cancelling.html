Noise Cancelling

Noise Cancelling

When it comes to creating a quiet home atmosphere, it’s what’s on the inside (of your doors) that counts.

Terms to keep in mind while you shop

Hollow Core

Spoiler alert: hollow core doors aren’t hollow inside. They’re actually filled with a corrugated honeycomb recycled material. This filler makes them lightweight, light on your wallet...but light on noise cancelling.

*Product availability varies upon region.

Solid Core

Solid core doors feel heavy in your hand, like solid wood. They’re filled with a low-density eco-friendly material that reduces sound transmission by 50 percent.

*Product availability varies upon region.
