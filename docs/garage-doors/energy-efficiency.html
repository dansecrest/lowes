Energy Efficiency

Do you want your garage to be well insulated? It all comes down to the door construction.

How construction layers affect energy efficiency

Construction layers

The more construction layers your door has and the thicker it is, the greater the R-value will be which can help maintain your garage’s temperature. This is important if the garage is attached to your home or used as a living space.

1-layer

Steel with no insulation. Since there is no insulation layer, there’s no R-value.

2-layer

For a more insulated, quieter garage, your base layer is steel, plus you have a layer of insulation. Good R-values.

3-layer

This is the toughest, most energy-efficient option with the best noise reduction. Steel exterior plus insulation and an interior layer of steel. Best R-value.
