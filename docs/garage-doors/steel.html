Steel

Steel

Steel is popular due to its durability, easy maintenance and high-quality appearance. It can imitate the look of wood but lasts longer and is easier to maintain.

Here are more reasons people choose steel.

It lasts a long time.

It’s one of the strongest, most durable and reliable materials around.

*Product availability varies upon region.

It’s low maintenance.

You’ll spend more time admiring your steel garage door than maintaining it.

*Product availability varies upon region.

It’s attractive.

The high-quality appearance of steel features state of the art wood grain embossment.

*Product availability varies upon region.
