Color

Color

The perfect garage door color can put the finishing touch on your home. Your color options will vary depending on your material and style.

See the benefits of painting color

Steel

Steel doors go through a five-step finishing process, giving them four layers of protection. A wide selection of colors are available for steel doors, from white to wood grain and even two-tone paint combinations.

*Product availability varies upon region.

Aluminum

Aluminum frames come in a variety of colors that can really make your house pop.

*Product availability varies upon region.

Wood

Wood doors come unfinished and need to be painted or stained once they’re installed.

*Product availability varies upon region.

Vinyl

Vinyl comes in UV-resistant white but can be easily painted over in any color.

*Product availability varies upon region.

DIY color tip:

If you’d like to paint your garage door, be sure to use exterior latex paint. Note: painting your garage door will void your manufacturing paint & finish warranty.

*Product availability varies upon region.
