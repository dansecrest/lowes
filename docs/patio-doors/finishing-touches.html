Finishing Touches

Don’t treat these details as afterthoughts. This is where style and function collide.

Finishing touches make a world of difference

Add on the charm.

If you’re serious about "old-world" charm, a few well-placed clavos and corner blocks can take your door from run-of-the-mill to Renaissance.

*Product availability varies upon region.

Choose hassle-free blinds.

Blinds can be troublesome between kids, pets and dust, but they provide much-needed shade. Blinds installed between the glass give you fingertip control without any of the hassle.

*Product availability varies upon region.

A polished look hinges on hardware.

Choosing the right hardware for your door comes down to style as much as function. Do you want a handle that’s sleek and mostly hidden or one that stands out? Do you want hinges that contrast or blend with your frame?

*Product availability varies upon region.
