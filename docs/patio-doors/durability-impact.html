Durability & Impact

Durability & Impact

From hurricane debris to the decibels of a passing airplane, there are ways to measure what can get through your door.

Here are some things to consider when you shop

Impact resistance

Depending on your building code, you may be required to have impact-resistant glass. This type of glass has a layer of polymer between the glass that can withstand wind, water and debris. Plus, it won’t shatter if it breaks.

Noise reduction

The number of panes, types of coating and layers of insulation all factor into how much noise can travel through your door. A door’s Sound Transmission Class (STC) rating will tell you how many decibels make it through the threshold.
