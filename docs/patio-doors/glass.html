Glass

Glass

Elevate your space with handcrafted decorative patio door glass that adds texture, privacy and personality.

Picture your patio with these glass options

Decorative glass

Unexpected glass details, like beveling, can enhance your patio’s atmosphere. The design you choose creates depth, contrast, and can add a certain amount of privacy.

*Product availability varies upon region.

Clear vs. textured

Clear glass is great when you have a stellar view from your patio. If your view comes second to privacy, glass textures like rain glass and granite glass add distinction and distortion.

*Product availability varies upon region.

Privacy rating

The higher the privacy rating the more privacy the glass offers. Zero means it’s transparent. Ten is the most opaque option, blurring the view  from outside to give you more privacy.

Grilles

Low-maintenance internal grilles add a nice design element, while external grilles have a more substantial, traditional look. Removable grilles offer easy snap-on, snap-off versatility.

*Product availability varies upon region.
