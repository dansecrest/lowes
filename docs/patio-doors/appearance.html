Appearance

Appearance

From the frame to the finish, your patio door can be customized to reflect your personal style.

Consider how style affects your door’s appearance

Frame

To complete your patio door’s look, you can add trim or a customized casing pattern for a bold and refined look.

*Product availability varies upon region.

Panel design

Do you want a traditional panel design, like French doors? Or a more contemporary design? How will the panels complement the frame material? Will they be flat or raised to match existing molding?

*Product availability varies upon region.

Color

Your color options will vary based on your frame material. Generally, darker colors absorb heat while lighter colors reflect heat. It might be something to consider if you live in an area with extreme temps.

*Product availability varies upon region.

Texture

Want your patio to set a precedent for modern minimalists? Opt for smooth metal textures. Want your patio to have that old-world charm? The distressed wood look should set the tone.

*Product availability varies upon region.

Protective finishes

Some exterior door frames come with a protective finish already. These finishes make maintenance a breeze and help prevent fading and rusting. Pre-primed options are also available for do-it-yourself painting or staining.

*Product availability varies upon region.
