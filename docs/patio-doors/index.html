If you go DIY, here’s how to measure your patio door.

Width

Measure distance between the side door jambs at three points: the top, middle and bottom of the patio door opening.

Height

Next, measure the distance between the top of the head jamb to the top of the bottom sill at three points: far left, center, and far right.

Depth

Measure the width of the jamb, from edge to edge, across the existing door frame. Ignore any door trim.
