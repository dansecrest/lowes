Fiberglass

Fiberglass

Fiberglass doors are durable, low maintenance and can even look like wood or steel.

Why choose fiberglass patio doors?

It’s durable.

Do you live in a particularly hot, cold, wet or dry climate? Fiberglass is designed to withstand both ends of the weather spectrum without warping or rotting. And it won’t dent, either.

*Product availability varies upon region.

It’s low maintenance.

Because of its baked-on protective finish, you won’t need to refinish or repaint it as it ages. It’s also easy to clean.

*Product availability varies upon region.

It’s versatile in style.

Right down to the knots, cracks and colors, fiberglass can be made to look like real wood. Smooth white and paintable finishes also make it a dent-resistant steel alternative. Your guests will be none the wiser.

*Product availability varies upon region.

Next, let’s discuss the benefits of vinyl.

Vinyl is a low-maintenance material formulated to look great for years to come.
