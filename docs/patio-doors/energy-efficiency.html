Energy Efficiency

Energy Efficiency

Energy efficiency matters if you’re looking to save money on bills or maximize the comfort of your home.

Here are terms to remember while you shop

Triple pane & low-E glass

Triple pane glass can be filled with gas that makes it hard for heat and humidity to pass through, which helps you cut your energy costs. Low-E glass has an invisible metallic coating that keeps heat inside during winter and outside during summer. Both types of glass will help cut your energy costs.

*Product availability varies upon region.

UV coating fade protection

Vinyl patio doors are always UV-coated to protect against fading. You can also get UV-coated glass that acts like sunscreen for furniture inside your home.

*Product availability varies upon region.

U-factor & Solar Heat Gain Coefficient (SHGC)

The lower the U-factor, the less heat and cold can transfer through the door. A lower SHGC means there are fewer UV rays passing through the glass. They’re both helpful factors in finding doors that meet ENERGY STAR® requirements for your region.

Screens

To take advantage of great weather and save on heat or air, a screen will allow you to open your patio door while keeping bugs out.

*Product availability varies upon region.
