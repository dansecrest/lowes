Folding

These doors are designed for large openings and fold away neatly to blur the line between indoors and outdoors.

Take a closer look at folding patio doors

Double

Double folding doors fold away nicely to make space for furniture and foot traffic. You can fold both doors away to the same side of the doorway or fold one to each side.

*Product availability varies upon region.

Multi

The more panels you have, the more natural light fills your indoor space. These can even fold around 90 degree corners to maximize the width of your patio opening.

*Product availability varies upon region.
