Sliding

These are easy to open and give you good ventilation and open horizontally or vertically.

View all of our sliding windows

Single-hung

If only one panel of glass moves up and down, we call that a single-hung window. It doesn’t provide as much ventilation as a double-hung, but if you have multiple single-hungs in a room, you get a nice cross-breeze.

*Product availability varies upon region.

Double-hung

If both panels of glass move up and down, we call that double-hung. They’re easier to to clean than single-hung and give better ventilation since hot air goes out the top panel and cool air comes in through the bottom panel.

*Product availability varies upon region.

Horizontal Sliding

If your window is in an area where sliding a window open vertically might be a challenge, try a horizontal sliding window.

*Product availability varies upon region.
