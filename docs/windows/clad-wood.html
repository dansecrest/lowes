Clad-wood & Wood

Clad-wood & Wood

With clad-wood you can enjoy the character of wood and the energy-efficiency of vinyl, fiberglass or aluminum.

Why choose clad-wood or wood windows?

Clad-wood

You have free reign over the stain and finish of the interior wood frame, while the weather-resistant exterior frame gives you peace of mind.

*Product availability varies upon region.

Wood

While wood windows require a bit of maintenance, they go a long way in preserving the character of historic homes.

*Product availability varies upon region.

Next up, aluminum windows.

Aluminum windows are known for being low maintenance, weather-resistant and easy to open.
