Combinations

Combinations

You can combine different types of windows to complement a home’s overall design. Here’s how to pull off some of the most popular architectural styles.

View all of our combination windows

You can mix and match common window shapes and sizes to create unique designs

Keep ventilation in mind when designing your window combinations.

Make sure all the windows in each combination are the same depth.

Victorian

This look is all about balancing bold statements with intricate details. The more ornate the better.

Recommended windows:
  • Arch windows
  • Double-hung
  • Bay
  • Bow
*Product availability varies upon region.

Modern

Open spaces. Clean lines. Lots of natural light. It’s all about bringing the outdoors inside.

Recommended windows:
  • Gliding
  • Picture
  • Bow
  • Special shapes
*Product availability varies upon region.

Craftsman

The curb appeal of a Craftsman bungalow home comes from its balanced sections of wood, brick and glass.

Recommended windows:
  • Single-hung
  • Double-hung
  • Casement
  • Awning
*Product availability varies upon region.
