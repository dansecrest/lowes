Glass Options

Glass Options

We could write a book just about glass options, but instead we’ll just show you some of our favorites.

Grille type & patterns

Grilles come in varying widths and materials.

Exterior grilles offer a more traditional look while interior grills are low maintenance and easy to clean.

You can choose from existing patterns or create your own design.

Tinted

These days there are tinted glass and even glass adhesive films that block harsh UV rays and sun glare. You can have your pick of hues like bronze, platinum, silver and blue.

*Product availability varies upon region.

Beveled

If your window is the focal point in a room, beveled glass is a great way to elevate your space. You can keep it simple or create a true work of art inside your window frame.

*Product availability varies upon region.

Obscured

If you’d like natural light to fill a room but maintain your privacy, like, say, in a bathroom, we recommend choosing obscured glass.

*Product availability varies upon region.

Textured

Add natural light, privacy and personality with textured glass. It’s a nice detail that can create a striking focal point in a room. You can even mix and match multiple textures on a single pane.

*Product availability varies upon region.
