Wood

Wood doors are crafted to complement your home’s architectural style and personal aesthetic.

Why choose a wood entry door?

The craftsmanship

Wood has been the door material of choice throughout the ages and across the globe. It’s strong and secure yet warm and inviting. Centuries of craftsmanship have been passed down and improved upon in today’s doors.

*Product availability varies upon region.

The easy fixes

Scratches happen. On a metal door, this could be a costly and complicated fix. In a wood door, scratches can be easily repaired in no time.

*Product availability varies upon region.

The innovation

Wood entry doors have a floating panel that allows the door to contract and expand to weather extremes and prevents splitting and cracking.

*Product availability varies upon region.
