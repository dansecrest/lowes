Safety & Security

Safety & Security

From multi-point hardware to fire-resistance, you don’t ever have to second guess your entry door’s security.

Things to consider while you shop

Fire-rated doors

A door and its frame together create a fire-rated system. Steel can be fire-rated as high as 90 minutes, while fiberglass is usually rated for 20 minutes.

*Product availability varies upon region.

Multi-point locking systems

In addition to standard deadbolts and foot bolts, you can have a multi-point lock system installed. Its three secure locking points of engagement on the door provide added security—and peace of mind. Deadbolts and foot bolts are also great lock options.

*Product availability varies upon region.
