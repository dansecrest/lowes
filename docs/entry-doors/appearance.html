Appearance

Appearance

From the frame to the finish, your entry door can be customized to reflect your personal style.

Consider your entry door’s appearance

Frame

Frames can be painted to match your door, sidelights and transoms. They’re available in a variety of materials and performance factors, like rot resistance.

*Product availability varies upon region.

Panel design

Do you want one big panel or several smaller panels? Will your door have glass and panels, or no glass at all for more privacy? You can design your door to highlight your home’s architectural style and personal taste.

*Product availability varies upon region.

Color

Your color options will vary based on your door material, whether paintable or stainable. Generally, darker colors absorb heat while lighter colors reflect heat. Something to consider if you live in an area with extreme temps or if the door is exposed to direct sunlight.

*Product availability varies upon region.

Texture

Going for sleek and modern? Opt for smooth textures. Want your entryway to have that old-world charm? Distressed wood textures should set the tone.

*Product availability varies upon region.

Protective finishes

You have the option of ordering a professionally finished door with a protective paint or stain that looks sharp and will last for years. Or, you can order it unfinished and customize the look any way you want.

*Product availability varies upon region.
