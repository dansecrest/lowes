Sidelights & Transoms

Don’t make your door do all the heavy lifting. Add sidelights and transoms to enhance your entryway’s look.

What to know about sidelights and transoms

Sidelights

Sidelights are the windows that flank either side of your entry door. You can add one or two, and customize the size, shape and glass type to complement your door.

*Product availability varies upon region.

Transoms

Transoms are the windows that go over the door. Like sidelights, they can also be customized to complement and even alter your door’s appearance. For instance, transoms can create an arched entrance around a rectangular door.

*Product availability varies upon region.
